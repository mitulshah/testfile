Pathology (Blood testing)
SRL Phadke
SRL Diagnostics and Dr. Avinash Phadke’s Lab is part of India’s leading diagnostics chain SRL, having more than 200 laboratories across the country. Equipped with state of the art facilities for more than 12000 specialized tests, SRL is accredited with the prestigious NABL certification. Spearheaded in Navi Mumbai by one of Vashi’s leading pathologists, Dr. Suresh Tekchandani, SRL Phadke and Suasti together strive to ensure complete accuracy and reliability of results and quickest turnaround times for patients
