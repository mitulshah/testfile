Sneh Jain
An MBA from IIM Ahmedabad and B.Tech Pharma from UDCT, Mumbai, Sneh worked with McKinsey and Co. on projects all over the world and was living the life of ahigh flying consultant (literally with all the travel). However, the itch to do his own biznuz (arising from his Marwadi genes) finally overpowered any corporate aspirations and led to the birth of Suasti Family Medical Centre. His expertise, especially in healthcare, helps power Suasti to greater heights. More importantly, do not rub him the wrong way. Not only does he have martial arts training of more than 6 years, he regularly skydives and bungees to proclaim ‘No Fear’.
Aditi Handa
Our inhouse master of all trades, Aditi has trained as a psychologist, HR expert and even as a baker. In short, a lethal concoction of skillsets which helps drive everyone around her. First, she successfully established India’s first branded college merchandise store – The Wimwian at IIM Ahmedabad. However, life had something else in store for her and she decided to join Sneh and Siddharth in starting Suasti. Her HR policies ensure employees at Suasti are the most satisfied. A stickler for systems, Aditi has everything planned out in excel – right from patient waiting times to email reminders. Nothing escapes her all seeing eye.
Siddharth Handa
The most eligible bachelor of Suasti, Siddharth was, we guess, the inspiration for ‘3 Idiots’. He spent his mechanical engineering time designing racing cars and actually learning about machines rather than learn by rote. A job at Orchid Pharmaceuticals couldn’t keep his inquisitive nature satisfied and hence, he decided to ask questions to himself instead. The epitome of inquisitiveness, he will grill you with the 5 Ws, How and more. The excruciating level of detail going into everything at Suasti has Siddharth’s signature on it. Further, his jokes and wisecracks ensure there is never a dull moment around him.
